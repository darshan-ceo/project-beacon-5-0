# Case Stage Management Checklist

## Pre-Stage Advancement Checklist

### Before Moving to Any New Stage
- [ ] All mandatory documents uploaded
- [ ] Previous stage tasks completed
- [ ] Client approval obtained (if required)
- [ ] SLA requirements met
- [ ] Quality review completed

## Stage-Specific Checklists

### Scrutiny to Demand
- [ ] GSTIN verification completed
- [ ] Assessment basis identified
- [ ] Demand calculation verified
- [ ] Notice period compliance checked
- [ ] Draft demand notice reviewed

### Demand to Adjudication
- [ ] Demand notice served
- [ ] Client response received
- [ ] Supporting documents compiled
- [ ] Hearing date scheduled
- [ ] Adjudicating officer assigned

### Adjudication to Appeals
- [ ] Adjudication order received
- [ ] Appeal grounds identified
- [ ] Appeal fee calculated
- [ ] Time limitation verified
- [ ] Appeal memorandum drafted

### Appeals to GSTAT
- [ ] First appeal order received
- [ ] Technical grounds established
- [ ] GSTAT jurisdiction confirmed
- [ ] Application fee deposited
- [ ] Authorized representative appointed

### GSTAT to High Court
- [ ] GSTAT order received
- [ ] Substantial question of law identified
- [ ] High Court jurisdiction verified
- [ ] Writ petition drafted
- [ ] Court fee calculated

### High Court to Supreme Court
- [ ] High Court judgment received
- [ ] Constitutional question identified
- [ ] Leave to appeal obtained
- [ ] SLP petition prepared
- [ ] Supreme Court registry requirements met

## Common Prerequisites by Stage

### Document Requirements
**Scrutiny**: Show cause notice, supporting documents
**Demand**: Demand notice, computation sheets
**Adjudication**: Hearing notices, submissions
**Appeals**: Appeal memorandum, grounds
**GSTAT**: Application, technical grounds
**HC**: Writ petition, legal grounds
**SC**: SLP petition, constitutional questions

### Timeline Compliance
- **Appeals**: 90 days from adjudication order
- **GSTAT**: 120 days from appellate order
- **High Court**: 180 days from GSTAT order
- **Supreme Court**: 90 days from HC judgment

### Fee and Deposit Requirements
- **Appeals**: Pre-deposit as per rules
- **GSTAT**: Application fee + hearing fee
- **High Court**: Court fee as per valuation
- **Supreme Court**: Filing fee + additional fee

## Quality Gates

### Mandatory Reviews
- [ ] Legal accuracy verification
- [ ] Factual consistency check
- [ ] Documentation completeness
- [ ] Timeline compliance
- [ ] Client communication

### Approval Workflow
1. **Paralegal Review**: Initial document check
2. **Senior Associate Review**: Legal accuracy
3. **Partner Approval**: Strategic decisions
4. **Client Consent**: Major procedural steps

## Risk Assessment

### High-Risk Indicators
- [ ] Tight statutory timelines
- [ ] Complex legal issues
- [ ] High financial exposure
- [ ] Multiple jurisdictions
- [ ] Precedent-setting matters

### Mitigation Strategies
- Early case planning
- Expert consultation
- Client communication
- Backup filing strategies
- Alternative dispute resolution
